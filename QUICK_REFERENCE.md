## ğŸ“§ Email Templates Quick Copy

### Venue Inquiry Subject Lines
```
[Band Name] - Booking Inquiry for [Month/Year]
Metal Show Booking: [Band Name] available [Date Range]
```

### First Email Key Points
- Introduce band
- Attach tech rider
- Provide music/video links
- Suggest 2-3 dates
- Make it easy for them

### Follow-up (After 1-2 Weeks)
- Brief and friendly
- Reference previous email
- Restate interest
- One follow-up is enough

## ğŸ†˜ Troubleshooting

### "Venue isn't responding"
- Follow up once after 2 weeks
- Move on if still no response
- Try again in 3-6 months

### "Low turnout at show"
- Analyze promotion efforts
- Build partnerships with local bands
- Focus on fan building, not venue
- Still give 110%

### "Running out of merch"
- Check `/docs/templates/merch-inventory.md`
- Contact vendor ASAP
- Consider pre-orders for new items

### "Behind on finances"
- Block 30 min to catch up
- Use template format
- Keep receipts moving forward
- Set weekly reminder
